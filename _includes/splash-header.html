HARDCAML

In 2022, we, the team who develops Hardcaml (Andy Ray, Ben Devlin, Fu Yong Quah, and Rahul Yesantharao) participated in the ZPrize competition. We competed in the Multi-Scalar Multiplication (MSM) and Number Theoretic Transform (NTT) tracks, winning the MSM FPGA track.

Read on to find out more about our submissions and view the code on github.
